Object of the game

To produce 12 high-scoring poker hands from the horizontal rows, vertical columns and diagonals.

Game rules

Place the cards from the stock, one by one, on any empty space of your choosing. Only one card per grid field is allowed. Once placed, a card cannot be moved.

Scoring

Your final score for the game is the total of the scores for your 12 poker hands.
